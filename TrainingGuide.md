# Buenas Shoppers InventoryPro Training Plan (6-Day Schedule)

This training plan is designed to onboard users to the InventoryPro system, covering everything from basic navigation to advanced financial management and reporting. It is structured to build knowledge logically, starting with setup and master data before moving to transactional workflows.

**Target Audience:** Warehouse Managers, Sales Staff, Administrators, and Procurement Officers.

---

## **Day 1: System Basics & Master Data Setup**
**Objective:** Familiarize users with the interface and establish the core data required for operations.

### **Morning Session: Introduction & Configuration**
1.  **System Overview**
    *   Login/Logout procedures.
    *   Navigation Bar & Dashboard overview.
    *   Understanding the multi-branch and multi-warehouse structure.
    *   Profile Settings (updating passwords, user preferences).
2.  **Organization Setup (Admin Only)**
    *   **Branches:** Adding and configuring branch locations.
    *   **Warehouses:** Creation of storage locations and bins (if applicable).
    *   **Users & Roles:** Creating user accounts and assigning permissions (RBAC).

### **Afternoon Session: Entity Master Data**
3.  **Partners Management**
    *   **Suppliers:** Creating supplier profiles, setting payment terms.
    *   **Customers:** Creating customer profiles, managing credit limits.
4.  **Product Catalog (Part 1)**
    *   Creating new Products.
    *   Understanding Basic Information (names, SKUs, barcodes).
    *   **Critical Concept:** Units of Measure (UOMs) â€“ Base units vs. Purchase/Sales units.

---

## **Day 2: Advanced Product Management & Inventory Basics**
**Objective:** Master the product catalog and understand how inventory is tracked.

### **Morning Session: Advanced Products**
1.  **Product Catalog (Part 2)**
    *   Product Categories & Grouping.
    *   Pricing strategies (Cost price vs. Selling price).
    *   Setting Reorder Levels and Safety Stock.
2.  **Costing**
    *   How Average Costing is calculated in the system.

### **Afternoon Session: Inventory Initialization**
3.  **Opening Stock**
    *   Importing or manually entering initial stock levels.
    *   Assigning stock to specific warehouses.
4.  **Inventory Maintenance**
    *   Using the `Data Maintenance` module.
    *   Stock Adjustments (Damage, Loss, Corrections).
    *   Viewing Stock functionality (by Warehouse, by Branch).

---

## **Day 3: The Procurement Cycle (Purchasing)**
**Objective:** Learn how to replenish stock efficiently and manage incoming goods.

### **Morning Session: Ordering**
1.  **Purchase Orders (PO)**
    *   Creating a new Purchase Order.
    *   Selecting suppliers and adding products.
    *   Understanding PO Statuses (Draft, Pending, Approved).
    *   Emailing/Printing POs for suppliers.

### **Afternoon Session: Receiving**
2.  **Receiving Vouchers (GRN)**
    *   Converting a PO into a Receiving Voucher.
    *   Partial Receiving (receiving less than ordered).
    *   **Quality Check:** Verifying product condition upon receipt.
    *   Confirming stock increase in the `Inventory` module.
    *   Handling returns to suppliers (if applicable).

---

## **Day 4: Sales Operations & Point of Sale (POS)**
**Objective:** Handle outgoing stock through various sales channels.

### **Morning Session: Wholesale/B2B Sales**
1.  **Sales Orders**
    *   Creating Sales Orders for wholesale customers.
    *   Checking stock availability during order entry.
    *   Applying discounts and tax.
    *   Fulfillment/Dispatch of Sales Orders.

### **Afternoon Session: Retail/Counter Sales**
2.  **Point of Sale (POS)**
    *   Launching the POS interface.
    *   Scanning items / Quick search.
    *   Handling different payment methods (Cash, Card, Credit).
    *   Processing walk-in customer transactions.
    *   Receipt printing.
3.  **Sales History**
    *   Viewing past transactions.
    *   Handling customer returns/refunds.

---

## **Day 5: Financial Management (AR/AP)**
**Objective:** Manage the flow of money related to inventory transactions.

### **Morning Session: Payables & Expenses**
1.  **Accounts Payable (AP)**
    *   Reviewing outstanding supplier balances.
    *   Linking Receiving Vouchers to Supplier Bills.
    *   Recording outgoing payments.
2.  **Expense Management**
    *   Recording operational expenses (Rent, Utilities, etc.) in the `Expenses` module.

### **Afternoon Session: Receivables & Collections**
3.  **Accounts Receivable (AR)**
    *   Tracking customer debt/credit.
    *   Aging analysis (Who owes money and for how long).
    *   Recording customer payments against invoices.
    *   Credit limit enforcement during sales.

---

## **Day 6: Analytics, Reporting & Administration**
**Objective:** Gain insights from data and maintain system health.

### **Morning Session: Business Intelligence**
1.  **Dashboard & Alerts**
    *   Interpreting Dashboard metrics (Sales today, Low stock alerts).
    *   Actioning on `Alerts` (Low stock, Reorder points).
2.  **Reports Module**
    *   **Inventory Reports:** Stock Valuation, Movement History.
    *   **Sales Reports:** Best-selling items, Sales by Rep/Branch.
    *   **Financial Reports:** Profit & Loss estimates, Cash flow summaries.

### **Afternoon Session: Review & Maintenance**
3.  **System Maintenance**
    *   Regular data integrity checks.
    *   Backup procedures (if manual intervention is required).
4.  **Practical Assessment / Q&A**
    *   Simulated workflow test: "Create a product, buy it, sell it, and check the profit."
    *   Troubleshooting common errors.
    *   Feedback session and Certification (optional).
